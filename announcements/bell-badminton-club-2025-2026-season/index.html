Bell Badminton Club 2024/2025 Season

Hello Bell Badminton Shuttlers,

Hope everyone had a nice summer.

The Bell Badminton Club 2025/2026 season will be starting on Friday, September 19, 2025 at 6:00 p.m We play every Friday from 6:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.; however, we kindly ask everyone to stop playing at 9:45 p.m. to help clean-up and to put away the equipment.

Cancellations

Please make note of the following cancellations where there will be NO badminton play:

  • December 26, 2025
  • January 2, 2026
  • March 20, 2026
  • April 3, 2026
  • April 17, 2026
  • May 1, 2026

The last day of play will be on May 22, 2026. This gives us 30 days of play.

Our club is currently full and will not be accepting anymore new members until further notice.

Guest Drop-Ins

For the first two weeks, there will be no guests drop-ins. Starting October 3, 2025, 4 guests will be accepted per night. The guest fee is $10 per drop-in. Guests are asked to contact bellbclub@gmail.com if they are interested to drop-in. Guests are only accepted after they receive a confirmation from the club to drop-in and their payment has been received.

Scent Free

Bell Badminton Club fosters a scent free environment. Please do not wear perfume or any scented product as some members have scent allergies.

Looking forward to a wonderful playing season with everyone!

See you all soon, Bell Badminton Club
